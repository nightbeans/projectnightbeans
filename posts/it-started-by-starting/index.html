It started by starting

May 03, 2024

What to do if there is nothing?
What the hell do we do?
Let’s just eat.
Let’s just sit.
There is nothing, right.
There is nothing right.
Let’s do nothing, let’s do.
No one will remember.
If nothing ever happened,
nothing ever will.
I think something just showed up,
it started by starting.
What do we do if there’s something?
What on earth will we do?
There’s something, right.
There’s something right.
Let’s do something, let’s do.
Someone will remember.
If something started happening,
something surely will.
Let’s do what we can,
let’s eat,
let’s sit.
I think something showed up,
it started by starting.
It proved something right,
it showed us that nothing,
was never in sight.
