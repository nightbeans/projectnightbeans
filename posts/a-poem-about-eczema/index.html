A poem about eczema

April 27, 2024

Are you serious?
Who cares about your eczema?
So what if it’s hot?
So what if it’s itchy?
No one cares.
Quite right.
What would even make this a poem?
Ask me about it to my face.
Maybe a five-year-old will reply.
He usually does.
Leave it alone,
you’ll only make it worse.
