I’m writing in a notebook

August 20, 2024

I haven’t given up on this. I’m writing in a notebook. I thought I would have more to say about things in the real world but I’m writing a bunch of nonsense made up stories. No plan it’s just strange stuff that’s popping up.

It’s very nerve-wracking to think of putting any of that to a blog - I may yet - but not at present.

I will endeavour to write a blog post that I am proud of soon. But this is checking in to say that that practice is not abandoned, its just nervily rehearsing lines backstage.
