Further down

April 07, 2024

Time on the
surface.
Short chopping
breaths.
Quick flying
kicks.
They love to
distract.
I long to
detract.
It is there at the
bottom.
It is always further
down.
If only I were
earnest.
I hope I
mean,
what I
say.
