Who knows

April 03, 2024
→ ←

It’s dark out,
who is home?
Let them come in,
they are alone.
It’s a cliff they are on,
I don’t think they know.
There really is nowhere else to go,
let’s wait.
We’ll see.
Which of them will come
to me.
