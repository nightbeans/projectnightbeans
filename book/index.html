Eyelids

Conor and Calum have long-worshipped their sister, Ella. But something in her has changed, and for the first time, they're afraid of her.

What follows forces the boys apart; Conor travelling inward to his 'deep' for refuge, while Calum remains in the world above.

They each have their guardians, the people they must trust to survive.
