Mr Armitage fell then, clutching his neck. Calum figured an asthma attack. Wesley figured Thadodaho. Mary figured pretence. He coughed and sputtered. It very quickly didn’t look like an asthma attack. It was less like he couldn’t get enough air in his lungs, but like his lungs were filling with something else.
