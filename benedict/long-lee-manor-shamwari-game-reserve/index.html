The one that started it all. A once-in-a-lifetime trip with my Mum and Dad. A truly remarkable place and experience. Yes, we were fortunate to see the 'Big Five' most dangerous animals here in South Africa - lions, elephants, rhino, buffalo and even a leopard, as well as many other beautiful creatures.

But enough of all that! What stood out most was that most delectable meal I had each morning. Golden, rich yolks. Buttery, light, tangy hollandaise topped with chives, of course. Thick-cut well-done bacon from what must have been a very happy pig.

But what mattered most? What makes this the best eggs benedict I have ever had bar none? What made me order it three mornings in a row? The base. Sourdough toast? No! A beautiful english muffin? No! Some kind of interesting rye bread for the fiber-brigade? No!

A humble potato rosti. The best rosti ever made. It was, my friends, the perfect eggs benedict. Pure bliss. Perfect balance.
